## 1 定义
$z = x+yi$
- x：实部，记为 $x=Re\ z$
- y：虚部，记为 $y = lm\ z$

&emsp;
## 2 复数的运算
>加法运算

$z_1 \pm z_2 = (a_1 + b_1i) \pm (a_2 + b_2i) = (a_1 + a_2) \pm (b_1 + b_2)i$

>乘法运算

$z_1  z_2 = (a_1 + b_1i)\ (a_2 + b_2i) = (a_1a_2 - b_1b_2) + (a_1a_2 + b_1b_2)i$

&emsp;
>除法

$\frac{z_1}{z_2} = \frac{a_1 + b_1i}{a_2 + b_2i} = \frac{(a_1 + b_1i)(a_2 - b_2i)}{a_2^2 - b_2^2} (a_2b_2 \neq 0)$
